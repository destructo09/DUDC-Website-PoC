Welcome to the Club

We are a community of divers based in the Triangle area of NC. Whether you love searching for shark teeth, diving quarries, or just talking shop, you belong here.

🌊

Dive Together

Join group dives at local quarries, NC coast wrecks, and organized trips to warm-water destinations.

💬

Monthly Meetings

Every 2nd Tuesday at Dave & Buster's in Cary. Guest speakers, trip reports, and good company.

🤝

Community First

We support all local dive shops without competing. Cleanup dives, safety talks, and mentoring new divers.

Down Under Divers Club

The largest and longest-running independent scuba club in North Carolina. Bringing divers together since 1995.
