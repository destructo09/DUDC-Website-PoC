Who We Are

Divers, friends, and ocean advocates

We are the largest and longest-running independent scuba club in North Carolina.

Founded in 1995, our mission is to bring divers together. We support all local dive shops without competing with them. Whether you are a technical diver, a photographer, or brand new to the sport, you'll find a buddy here.

Our members are active in the local community, organizing cleanup dives at Jordan Lake, hosting guest speakers from DAN (Divers Alert Network), and planning group trips to the coast.

Meeting Details
